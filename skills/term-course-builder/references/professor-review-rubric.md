# Professor Review Rubric

Use this when synthesizing sources, reviewing a syllabus, or validating final lessons.

## Review stance

Act as an expert in generative AI, pedagogy, and practical systems design. Prefer clarity, scope control, and useful examples over exhaustive coverage.

## Questions to answer

- Is the module teaching a concept or just collecting notes?
- Is the worked example small but non-trivial?
- Does the theory come before construction?
- Does the course explain where the concept lives in a real system?
- Are required and optional components explicit?
- Do blocks and lessons form a progression?
- Are templates explained inside the learning flow?
- Are evals concrete enough to reveal failure?
- Is anything too broad and better split into another module?

## Required output for review

Return:

1. What works.
2. What is missing.
3. What is too broad.
4. What should be added before writing lessons.
5. Concrete edits or structure changes.

Do not praise vaguely. If something is weak, name the file, section, or phase that should change.
